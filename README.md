# nomi 记账本!

根据用户当前拥有的资金量追踪消费愿望清单。

## 功能：

- 双标签页：

- 购物：查看用户想要购买或考虑购买的商品列表。

- 资金：允许用户追踪当前资金状况，并考虑收入和债务。

- 人民币和美元切换功能。


# nomi Budgeting!

Train your spending wish list based on your current financial situation.

## Features:

- Dual Tabs:

- Shopping: View a list of items you want to buy or are considering buying.

- Funds: Allows you to track your current financial situation and consider income and debt.

- RMB/USD Switching Functionality
